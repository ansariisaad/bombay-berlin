Katharina Suckale.

A background in the film and TV business for more than 30 years, from sales and distribution at MK2 in Paris, Yash Raj Films in Mumbai to film and TV production in Europe, from Germany to Portugal, and from Canada to Africa. She developed series concepts for Canal+ France, ZDF, Disney+, and other channels and platforms for the European, Indian, and international markets. BBFP is based in Mumbai and Berlin. Our film “LOEV” was released worldwide on Netflix in 180 countries after 40+ major festivals from Tallinn Black Nights to SXSW and BFI Flare. “The Road to Mandalay” by Midi Z premiered at Venice Days, winning the Fedeora Award. The Taiwanese-French-German-Myanmar co-production went on to be screened at TIFF, Busan, and 40 other festivals and released in cinemas worldwide. The film is available on different platforms.

The latest feature film “Trijya-Radius” premiered at the Shanghai International Film Festival, screening at Tallinn Black Nights, Dhaka International Festival (Best Cinematography), Black Movie Festival, and Rainbow Festival (Best Film), winning the National and State Awards for Best Sound Design. The film is available on MUBI worldwide. Films like “The Idiot” were released on Disney+, Argo and Amazon US/UK or Jio Cinema in 2023. We are developing series and feature films for Disney+/Hotstar, Sony Liv, or channels like ZDF.

ARFI LAMBA Originally an engineer in the petrol-chemical industry, he joined the film business in 2011. With his impeccable skills of building up finances, handling operations, a keen eye for scripts, and know-how of the business of Indian and international cinema, he brings a very accurate judgment on what works in a particular market. He has often been a consultant, to various companies and colleagues on their projects, where his role has been to make films viable for international markets or commercial success back home in India.

His first feature as producer, LOEV (Tallinn Black Nights, SxSw, BFI Flare, Netflix,) is back with the company after completing the 5-year licensing deal with Netflix. His second feature as a co-producer was The Road To Mandalay (TIFF, BFI, Venice), by the very talented Midi Z. Next was Akshay Indikar’s debut, Trijya (Shanghai International Film Festival, Tallinn Black Nights) which not had four nominations in ANTA Awards but also won National and State Awards for Sound Design.

He is right now working on two international co-productions, two commercial Indian films, a documentary, and a short film. There is a series that has just completed development with one of the top platforms and is waiting for production greenlight soon.

Yogesh.

Yogesh began his professional journey after earning his degree from Oriental College of Commerce & Management (Mumbai University). His career kicked off in the restaurant industry, where he initially served as a cashier. After gaining valuable experience, he transitioned to the role of Manager in a Society Building, dedicating nearly three years to overseeing various responsibilities.

Currently, Yogesh has been an integral part of a Film Production company for the past three years, where he holds a key role as an Admin/Manager. In this capacity, he efficiently manages a spectrum of tasks, including accounting, operations, and more. Yogesh's diverse background showcases his adaptability and ability to thrive in different professional environments, making him a valuable asset to the dynamic field of film production.
